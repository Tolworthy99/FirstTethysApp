Brief Introduction

I grew up in several different towns in England. My family moved to Billingham when I was 10 and are still there. Billingham is where I first became interested in Geology at college. I attended St Michaels RC School and then SRC Bede Sixth Form College. I first came to the states to serve a LDS mission in Salt Lake City in 2011. Then I came back to Utah in 2014 to attend BYU and I have been here since. In 2015 I met Sara while working custodial and we were married a year later in the Oakland Temple.
